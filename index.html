Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the visa process take?

The visa process timeframe varies by destination, but on average it takes 3-8 months from the date of application. We always advise clients to start early to allow enough time for document preparation and embassy appointments.

Do you help with scholarships?

Yes! We offer guidance and support for travelers and students seeking scholarships, including information on available opportunities, application tips, and required documentation. While we can’t guarantee approval, our team helps maximize your chances with expert advice and up-to-date resources.
